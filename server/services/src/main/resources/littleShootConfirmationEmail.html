Welcome to LittleShoot, the easiest and most sophisticated file sharing program on the planet. Please click here to complete your registration.

LittleShoot gives you:
  1. Easy file sharing right in your browser
  2. Fast downloading
  3. Quick searching from other LittleShoot users as well as a growing list of other sources, including Flickr, YouTube, and Yahoo.

If you know how to use Google, you know how to use LittleShoot. It's that simple.

Unlike Google, though, LittleShoot files come from other users just like you. You get your files faster because you download from everyone on the network.

LittleShoot is open source and based on open standards - a file sharing program for the next generation Internet.
