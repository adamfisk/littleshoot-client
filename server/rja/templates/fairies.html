The Replacement Fairies: a television series

When the tooth fairy quit, the International Fairy Council had to find a replacement. They scoured fairy world and what they found shocked them: no one wanted the job. Then, the Copoddle siblings came forth. Gritt, a mischievous adventurer who adored human children, Dill, a bookworm annoyed at everything besides education, and Cookie, a lethargic, overweight little boy who did nothing but eat became the world's tooth fairy. These unlikely heros had one thing nobody else in fairy world had: they were willing to try.
