How does LittleShoot work? It's a browser plugin just like Flash, so LittleShoot runs on any web site that adds it. When you click on a BitTorrent link, for example, LittleShoot is the "handler" for that link and starts your download right up in the browser. When you close the browser window, LittleShoot stays running and downloading your files until you tell it not to using the system tray control on Windows (bottom right of your screen) or the status item control (top right buttons on your screen) on OSX. You can always check in on your downloads in the LittleShoot downloads tab at http://www.littleshoot/downloads.

You can also use LittleShoot to search for the files you want, whether on YouTube, IsoHunt, LimeWire, or Flickr. To search any time, just go to the search tab at http://www.littleshoot/search.

But first you need the plugin! Be sure to download and install it first.
