1
Download the LittleShoot Plugin

 This page describes all the steps to install LittleShoot. If you're familiar with installers, feel free to just run through the install yourself. Note the screenshots on your computer may differ slightly depending on your browser.

Your download should automatically start if you are on this page. If it does not, please click here.
2
Select Where to Download the Installer

 Save the installer to your computer. It's often a good idea to save it to your desktop, where you can find it easily afterwards.

3
Open the Installer

 Open the downloads window and click "Open" next to your LittleShoot download. You can also navigate to your desktop or wherever you saved LittleShoot and open it from there.

4
Follow the Setup Wizard

 Once the LittleShoot wizard appears, click "Install".



When the setup wizard is done, click "Finish".





At this point LittleShoot is already started and running in the background. A new browser window should open to the LittleShoot site automatically, although it may take a second while LittleShoot is starting. If it doesn't, click here.
